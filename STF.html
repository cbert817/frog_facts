How to Help

Reduce pesticide use
Pesticides are harmful to frog populations, so try to use natural alternatives or reduce their use in your garden or farm.
Create a frog-friendly habitat
Provide places for frogs to hide and breed, such as ponds, wetlands, and vegetation.
Reduce pollution
Avoid dumping chemicals or litter into streams, rivers, or lakes, as this can harm frog populations.
Support conservation organizations
Donate to or volunteer with conservation organizations working to protect and conserve frog populations.
Be a responsible pet owner
Don't release pet frogs into the wild, as this can introduce invasive species that can harm native frog populations.

Donate
